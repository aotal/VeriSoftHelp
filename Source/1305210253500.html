Merge Algorithm

The merged measurement is generated from the measurements #1 to #4. The shift of each measurement is always 5 mm (OCTAVIUS Detector 1000 SRS 2.5 mm).

The merged measurement covers the same area as measurement #1, i.e., the detector rows of the shifted measurements, which exceed the measuring area of measurement #1 (gray areas outside the wide border), are ignored (refer to Fig. Measuring area and origin of coordinates of the merged measurement).

The merged measurement has the same point of origin as measurement #1.
