Drift Suppression

(only for XDR devices)

When measuring scanned proton and heavy ion beams, the dose rate to be measured occurs during the short spot time of radiation at each individual chamber. The leakage current that exists during the remaining measuring time will be accumulated and thus will distort the dose value to be measured.

  • In order to avoid this effect, you can activate the drift suppression on the Measurement tab and enter a threshold for both measuring ranges, Low and High. Only values exceeding these thresholds will be considered during dose integration. Please ensure the following:
  • -The threshold should be twice the maximum noise and it has to be converted on the basis of the adjusted interval time (refer to the following example).
  • -The threshold has to be entered in the unit [Gy/interval].

Example:
maximum noise = 80 mGy/min
→threshold = 160 mGy/min
Interval time = 400 ms
