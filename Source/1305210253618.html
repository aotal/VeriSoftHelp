Discrete Crosshairs

  • By double-clicking the profile representation, discrete crosshairs will be displayed.
  • The values at the actual crosshairs position will be displayed in the status bar (refer to Fig. Results window).

Follow these steps to move the crosshairs in the graphic:

  • Left-click the graph and hold the mouse button down. When you move the mouse, the crosshairs will move from measurement point to measurement point on the profile. When you release the mouse button, the crosshairs will stop on the last measurement point.
  • Use the cursor keys ← and → (without the Shift key) to move the crosshairs from measurement point to measurement point.

  • When you position the mouse cursor over another measurement point and left-click, the crosshairs will be displayed on that measurement point.

The crosshairs are deactivated when closing the matrix or selecting the profile representation again.
