Pop-up Windows

When moving the cursor over the tabs (2), pop-up windows will be displayed on the left side of the compare window. These pop-up windows include the following functions:

  • -Compare tab
    displays a pop-up window for selecting the compare mode and adjusting the compare parameters (refer also to section Selecting the Compare Mode and Adjusting the Compare Parameters).
    If two dose volumes are loaded and the Gamma 3D compare mode is active, you can initiate the comparison of the dose distribution in the volume from this pop-up window (also refer to section Comparison of Dose Distribution in Volume).
  • -RTView tab
    displays a pop-up window for overlaying the patient anatomy with the dose distribution (refer also to section Overlaying the Patient Anatomy).
  • -DVH tab
    displays a pop-up window for calculation of DVH curves (DVH = dose volume histogram) from the data of an OCTAVIUS 4D measurement (refer also to section Dose Volume Histograms (Option)) and for comparison with the DVH curves from the TPS data.
    The pop-up window DVH is only displayed when a license for the "DVH 4D" option was purchased and activated.
