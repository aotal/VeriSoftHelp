Changing the Representation of the RTView Window

  • The patient anatomy can be represented in the following modes:
  • -2D
    two-dimensional representation, i.e., cut through the patient anatomy at the depth corresponding to the selected slice in the matrix windows
  • -3D
    three-dimensional representation, calculated from the CT series
  • -DRR
    digital reconstructed radiograph

The list box (1a) in the toolbar is used to select the representation mode.
