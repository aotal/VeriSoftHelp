Measuring

Hint

You should check whether the serial number of the detector array used matches the serial number shown in the status bar. The serial number shown in the status bar is read from the calibration file. VeriSoft will not check the connected hardware.

Hint

If a zeroing procedure is required, an appropriate message will be displayed and the measurement will not be started.

After the zeroing procedure the measurement will not be started automatically. In this case, initiate the measurement by clicking the Start button (or with F5) or by selecting the menu Measurement → Start.
  • The measurement will begin.
    VeriSoft will continuously read the accumulated measuring values and displays them.
    If measurement with the rotation unit or inclinometer is enabled, the gantry angle reading of the inclinometer will also be displayed.
  • When the set measuring time has elapsed, the measurement will automatically be terminated.
  • The measurement can also be terminated by clicking the Stop button (or with F6) or by selecting the menu Measurement → Stop.
     
  • When the measurement is terminated, you can accept the measurement result by clicking the Accept button. The measuring window will then be closed.
     
  • By clicking the Cancel button, the measuring window will be closed without accepting the measuring data.

 

  • Initiate the measurement again by clicking the Start button (or with F5) or by selecting the menu Measurement → Start.
