Compare Window

A compare result will be displayed in the compare window if two matrices (or dose volumes or lists of matrices) are loaded.

During loading, the matrices will be adapted as described in section Adaptation of Two Matrices during Loading.

Compare window
  1. 1Toolbar
    • aList box to select the compare result representation mode
    • bList box to select the display of further information
  2. 2Tab to display a pop-up window
  3. 3Graphical or tabular representation of the compare result
  4. 4Position of the profile displayed in the results window
  5. 5Legend
  6. 6Status bar with
    • aSelected compare mode
    • bCursor position in the Left-Right direction
    • cCursor position in the Target-Gun direction
    • dCompare value at the cursor position
    • eAbsolute dose difference at the cursor position
    • fRelative dose difference at the cursor position
