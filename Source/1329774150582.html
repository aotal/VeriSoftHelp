Display of Measurement Plane and Failed Points

In the patient anatomy, you can display the measurement plane and the failed points in the desired display plane and at the desired depth. You have to adjust the display plane and the depth in one of the matrix windows.

  • In one of the matrix windows, select the desired display plane by clicking the appropriate button (refer also to section Display Planes of Dose Volumes):
Coronal plane
Sagittal plane
Transversal plane
  • In the RTView window, the measurement plane or the failed points will be calculated in the adjusted display plane for a depth of 0 mm and displayed in the patient anatomy.
  • In one of the matrix windows, select the desired depth by means of the Slice slider.
  • In the RTView window, the measurement plane or the failed points will be calculated at the adjusted depth and displayed in the patient anatomy.
