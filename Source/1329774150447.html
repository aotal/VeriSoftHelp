Display of Beams

If beams are available in the loaded data set, you can display them in addition to the patient anatomy.

  • For this, click the desired beam in the Select visible Beams pane (2d).
  • The beam will be highlighted blue and in the Control Point of selected Beam list box the available control points will be displayed.
  • Click the beam again.
  • The check box will be selected and the beam will be displayed at the first control point.
  • In order to display the beam at another control point, select the desired control point in the Control Point of selected Beam list box.
  • If several beams are available, you can display them additionally. Repeat the described operating sequence for each beam.
