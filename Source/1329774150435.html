Display of Patient Contours

If patient contours (e.g., contour of the body, contours of body parts, or contours of organs) are available in the loaded data set, you can display them in addition to the patient anatomy.

  • For this, select the appropriate check boxes in the Select visible ROIs pane (2b).
  • The selected contours will be displayed in the appropriate colors.
    • -2D and DRR representation:
      The contours will be displayed as outlines at the current depth.
      If a contour is not cut at the current depth, it will not be displayed.
    • -3D representation:
      The contours will be displayed as structures.
