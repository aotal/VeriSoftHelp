Air Density Correction

The PTW detector arrays are vented ionization chamber arrays. For this reason, you have to perform an air density correction.

Hint

The OCTAVIUS Detector 1000 SRS is a liquid-filled detector array. In case of this detector array, you can only enter a calibration factor kuser and/or perform a cross calibration.

  • Select the Correction T and P option in the Measurement Parameters dialog:
Measurement Parameters dialog – Correction T and P
  • Enter the temperature and air pressure values.
 
  • -Temperature
    valid range:
    default:

  • (5 … 45) °C
    20 °C
 
  • -Air pressure
    valid range:
    default:

  • (600 … 1100) hPa
    1013.25 hPa
  • The correction factor kTP for air density correction will be calculated after the following formula:
 
 
T
temperature in measuring volume [°C]
 
P
air pressure at measuring site [hPa]
 
T0
reference temperature 20 °C or 22 °C
 
P0
reference air pressure 1013.25 hPa
Hint

The reference temperature for the measurement can be changed in the menu Tools → Measurement Options on the Measurement tab (refer to section Measurement Settings).

  • All further measurements are corrected by multiplication with the correction factors kTP, kUser, and kEnergy.
