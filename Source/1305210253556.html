Pop-up Windows

When moving the mouse cursor over the tabs (2), pop-up windows will be displayed on the left side of the matrix. These pop-up windows include the following functions:

  • -Slice tab
    is only available if a dose volume or a list of matrices is loaded.
    This tab displays a pop-up window with a slider. With this slider you can scroll through the slices of a dose volume or through a list of matrices (refer also to section Scrolling in Dose Volumes or Lists of Matrices).
  • -Contrast tab
    displays a pop-up window with a slider. With this slider you can change the grayscale display of the grayscale images (refer also to section Changing the Grayscale Display).
  • -Edit tab
    displays a pop-up window with the image processing functions for grayscale images and isodoses (refer to section Defining the Point of Origin of Coordinates up to section Rotating and Mirroring a Matrix).
