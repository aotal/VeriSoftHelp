Design of the RTView Window

RTView window
  1. 1Toolbar
    • aList box to select the representation mode
    • bList box to select the display of additional information
  2. 2Area for changing the representation:
    • aSelecting a POI (Point of Interest)
    • bDisplay of patient contours
    • cDisplay of isodoses
    • dDisplay of beams
    • eSpecial options depending on the representation mode
    • fChanging the grayscale display
  3. 3Button to reset the graphic window to the original size
  4. 4Representation of the patient anatomy and the selected representation options
  5. 5System messages
  6. 6Status bar
