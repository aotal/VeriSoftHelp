Safety Information

 
This is the safety alert symbol. It is used to alert the user to potential hazards. Obey all safety messages that follow this symbol to avoid any possible bodily injury or equipment damage.
  • All safety messages consist of the following components:
  • -Safety alert symbol and signal word
  • -Type of danger
  • -Source of danger
  • -Consequence
  • -Measures to prevent hazards.

Signal Words

Danger

Indicates an imminent hazard. If not avoided, the hazard will result in death or serious injury.

Warning

Indicates a hazard. If not avoided, the hazard can result in death or serious injury.

Caution

Indicates a potential hazard. If not avoided, the hazard can result in minor or moderate injury.

Notice

Indicates a potential hazard. If not avoided, the hazard can result in product damage.

Hint

Provides application tips or other useful information to ensure that you get the most from your equipment.

Safety Information Concerning the Software

Warning

Hazards originating from other system components.

Incorrect Operation!

Observe the safety information provided in the user manuals of the system components.

Warning

Use of VeriSoft by persons with inadequate qualifications.

Incorrect Operation!

Use VeriSoft only if you are familiar with the software and adequately trained or experienced in the use of such software and own the necessary decision-making competence to ensure proper use. This includes the following activities:

  • -determination of the measuring and analysis parameters
  • -determination of the tolerance levels
  • -selection of correction protocols according to different standards
  • -selection or generation of the correction tables according to different standards
  • -evaluation and plausibility check of measuring and analysis data provided by the software
  • -introduction of the software in the department
  • -as well as the instruction provided to the personnel on how to perform measurements.
Warning

The PC with VeriSoft is capable of interfering with the data transmission of other stations on the network.

Network Problem!

When operating the PC with VeriSoft via the Ethernet interface, connect it only to data networks if the safety of patients, the operator or other persons does not depend on the proper functioning of these networks.

Ensure that only the network administrator connects the PC to the data network.

For information on incorporating medical devices in clinical data networks, refer to the standards EN 80001-1 (IEC 80001-1).

Caution

Influences by other software.

Malfunction!

If software other than VeriSoft or software not approved by PTW-Freiburg is installed on the PC, PTW-Freiburg cannot be held liable for computer malfunctions resulting from this fact. Furthermore, PTW-Freiburg cannot guarantee the proper functioning if third-party software was previously installed on the PC and was not entirely removed.

For the proper functioning of VeriSoft, ensure that only one version of this software is installed on your PC.

Caution

Use in combination with unsuitable system components.

Incorrect Operation!

Install VeriSoft only on PCs that meet the requirements of the standard EN 60950-1 (IEC 60950-1) (UL 60950-1).

Make sure that the PC meets the minimum requirements according to section Minimum System Requirements.

Do not use the PC in the patient environment.

Use VeriSoft only in conjunction with the PTW devices listed in the section "Intended Use".
