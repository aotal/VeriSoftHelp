Consistency Check

If data sets are loaded in both matrix windows and in the RTView window, VeriSoft will check whether a correlation can be established between the comparative matrix and the RTView data set:

  1. VeriSoft will first check whether the StudyInstanceUIDs of both data sets match.
  • If they do not match, an appropriate message will be displayed. Additionally, a shortened message will be displayed permanently in the area (5) of the RTView window.
  1. If the StudyInstanceUIDs match, VeriSoft will also check whether the FrameOfReferenceUIDs of both data sets match.
  • If they do not match, an appropriate message will be displayed in the area (5) of the RTView window.
