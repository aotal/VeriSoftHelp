Pop-up Window

When moving the mouse cursor over the tab (2), a pop-up window with a traffic light will be displayed on the left side of the results window.

Warning

Using the evaluation data without a check.

Patient Hazard!

VeriSoft will evaluate the loaded matrices on the basis of the adjusted parameters and will show the result in the form of a traffic light.
It is essential to check the adjusted parameters before performing the verification. The compare mode settings will be displayed as a tooltip when moving the mouse cursor over the traffic light.

  • -If only one matrix is loaded, the traffic light is deactivated.
  • -If two matrices are loaded, the traffic light shows the result of the comparison of the matrices depending on the settings in the menu Tools → Options on the Compare tab (refer to section Compare Mode Options):
  • Green traffic light:
    The result is higher than the adjusted value or equal to the adjusted value for a green result.
  • Yellow traffic light:
    The result is higher than the adjusted value or equal to the adjusted value for a yellow result and lower than the adjusted value for a green result.
  • Red traffic light:
    The result is lower than the adjusted value for a yellow result.
  • When moving the mouse cursor over the traffic light, a tooltip with the compare mode settings will be displayed.
