Display Planes of Dose Volumes

When you have loaded a dose volume in a matrix window, you have the option to change the display plane of the dose volume. For the definition of the display planes, refer to Fig. Definition of the patient planes and the patient coordinate system.

For this, the button for selecting the display plane will be enabled in the toolbar (refer also to section Explanation of the Toolbar Icons). The button offers the following options:

Coronal plane
Sagittal plane
Transversal plane

After switching to another display plane, the slice of the dose volume at a depth of 0 mm in this direction will be displayed.

You can display the other slices of the dose volume by means of the slider in the pop-up window Slice.

Hint

The names of the profiles in the results window will not be changed if you switch to another display plane.
