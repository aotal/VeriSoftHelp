Correction Factor for the Beam Quality

For air density correction, you can additionally enter a correction factor for the beam quality.

The correction factors for the beam qualities are defined in a table.

  • Click the Change button in the Measurement Parameters dialog.
  • The Energy Calibration dialog will be displayed:
Energy Calibration dialog
  • Select the correction factor by selecting the appropriate check box.
Hint

VeriSoft will not check whether the adjusted and the actually used beam quality are identical.

Adding a Correction Factor

  • Enter the energy and the corresponding correction factor into the empty row of the table.
  • Confirm with the Enter key.
  • The new correction factor will be appended to the table.

Removing a Correction factor

  • Select the correction factor to remove by clicking the appropriate row.
  • Press the Del key.
  • The selected correction factor will be removed.
