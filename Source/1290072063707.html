Data Backup and Restoring a Backup

To avoid loss of data, e.g., in case of a computer crash, it is recommended to make data backups at regular intervals.

The data are located, e.g., in the directories
'<PTW documents>\VeriSoft\Data' and
'<PTW application files>\VeriSoft\VeriSoft\
BatchProcess'.

Hint

To avoid loss of data when moving data, it is recommended to do so using the Copy/Paste and Delete functions rather than Cut and Paste.
