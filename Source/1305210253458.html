Explanation of the Toolbar Icons

Hint

Please note that the visible buttons or selectable buttons depend on the selected detector array.

1. General Buttons

Save data
Delete displayed measuring curve and release the port
Start measurement (or press F5)
Pause measurement (or press F6)
Accept measuring data and close the measuring window
Abort measurement and close the measuring window

2. Button with Pick List

  • -The displayed icon shows the current selection.
  • -Clicking a button selects the next option of the button. The button will show the appropriate icon and the corresponding function will be performed.
  • -A menu showing all options will be displayed in each case by clicking the button. After selecting an option, the button will show the appropriate icon and the corresponding function will be performed.

Selecting the Measuring Parameters:

Measuring range Low
Measuring range High

Selecting the Orientation of the Rotation Unit

normal orientation of the rotation unit, i.e., gearbox of the rotation unit faces gun
rotation unit rotated by 180°, i.e., gearbox of the rotation unit faces target

Selecting the Inclinometer Mounting Position

The inclinometer is mounted at the front of the radiation unit.
The inclinometer is mounted on the back of the radiation unit (e.g., on Varian Halcyon™)
