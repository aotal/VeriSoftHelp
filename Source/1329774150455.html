Changing Representation in 3D View

Selecting the Representation

  • With the Set defined view list box (2e), you can select one of the following views for the three-dimensional representation of the patient anatomy:
  • -Front
  • -Back
  • -Left
  • -Right
  • -Top
  • -Bottom

Rotating the Representation

In 3D view, you can rotate the displayed patient anatomy.

  • Press and hold the left mouse button and move the mouse.
  • The patient anatomy will be rotated accordingly.
  • To reset to default, select the view again with the Set defined view list box.

Zooming in or Zooming out the Representation

In 3D view, you can zoom in or zoom out the displayed patient anatomy.

  • Press and hold the right mouse button and move the mouse.
  • The patient anatomy will be zoomed in or zoomed out.
  • To reset to default, select the view again with the Set defined view list box.

Changing the Colors and the Opacity

By means of the Rendering function (Rendering button), you change the colors of patient structures and their opacity.

Shortening the Display Area

By means of the Cropping function (Cropping button), you shorten the display area in the three axis directions.
