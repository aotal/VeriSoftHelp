Adding and Loading OCTAVIUS 4D Measurements

Hint

Adding and loading of OCTAVIUS 4D measurements is only possible if they have been measured with the same device.

You can add and load complete OCTAVIUS 4D measurements only. In this case, the selection of an angle range is not possible.

It is also possible to calculate an OCTAVIUS 4D dose volume for added OCTAVIUS 4D measurements in the following cases:

  • -non-coplanar irradiation
  • -irradiation of a rotation unit not positioned at the isocenter
  • -irradiation with different energies
  • -irradiation of a rotation unit not positioned at the isocenter with different energies

If you select several files with OCTAVIUS 4D measurements in the standard Open dialog, all measurement intervals from all measurements will be included in the calculation of the OCTAVIUS 4D dose volume.

For further information on loading OCTAVIUS 4D measurements, please refer to section Loading OCTAVIUS 4D Measurements and Calculating the OCTAVIUS 4D Dose Volume.
