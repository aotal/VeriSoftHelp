Defining the Zero Point of the Inclinometer

When you start a measurement with detector array and inclinometer, you first have to define the zero point of the inclinometer.

  • To do so, rotate the gantry to 0°.
  • Select the menu Measurement → Zero Inclinometer.
  • The current inclinometer position is defined as the zero point.
Hint

In case of a measurement with a detector array in a rotation unit, the zero point of the inclinometer is determined by means of the reference run of the rotation unit.
