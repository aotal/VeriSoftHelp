Setting the Measuring Range

Hint
  • Setting the measuring range is not possible for the following detector arrays:
  • -2D ARRAYseven29
  • -2D ARRAY XDR
  • Depending on the dose to measure, you can set the following measuring ranges:
  • -Range Low
    measuring range Low
  • -Range High
    measuring range High

Both measuring ranges depend on the set measurement speed.

  • The measuring range will be displayed
  • -as a tooltip by moving the mouse cursor over the appropriate button
  • -in the menu, by clicking the appropriate button
