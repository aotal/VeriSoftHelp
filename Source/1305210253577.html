Defining the Isocenter Automatically

With the Align Automatically function, the matrices or the dose volumes will be overlaid by means of an image comparison and thus the new isocenter of the comparative matrix will be calculated.

  • Select menu Edit → Align Automatically.
  • VeriSoft will calculate the translation vector and will display it in the Auto Alignment dialog for matrices and in the 3D Auto Alignment dialog for dose volumes:
Auto Alignment dialog for matrices
3D Auto Alignment dialog for dose volumes
  • Confirm the displacement with OK.
  • The isocenter of the comparative matrix will be translated. The display will be updated. The compare results will be recalculated and displayed.
  • The reference matrix remains unchanged.
