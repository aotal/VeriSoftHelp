Licensing Procedure

Licensing can be done in two ways:

  1. The licensing information can be entered during the installation. In this case, the software will automatically be licensed after the installation.
  2. If the data entered during the installation are invalid, an appropriate message will be displayed and a demo version of the software will be licensed that can be used for a period of 30 days. In this case, the licensing information can be entered at a later date via the PTW License Control Center.
Hint

The trial period of the demo version begins on the installation day.

Options, updates, and expired demo versions can also be licensed whenever necessary via the PTW License Control Center.
