Measuring the Depth Dose Curves with PTW Devices and PTW Software

For measuring the depth dose curves, PTW recommends using a PTW water phantom and the PTW measuring software MEPHYSTO mc².

  • Start the "Water Tank Scans" software from the MEPHYSTO Navigator.
  • Measure depth dose curves with the following settings (for further information on measuring refer to the MEPHYSTO mc² user manual):
    • -SSD (Source Surface Distance):
      • -when using the accessory Rotation Unit: 85 cm
      • -when using the accessory Rotation Unit SRS: 92 cm
    • -required field sizes (at isocenter with SID of 100 cm):
      • -when using the accessory Rotation Unit: 4 cm x 4 cm, 5 cm x 5 cm, 10 cm x 10 cm, 15 cm x 15 cm, and 26 cm x 26 cm
      • -when using the accessory Rotation Unit SRS: 4 cm x 4 cm, 5 cm x 5 cm, 8 cm x 8 cm, 10 cm x 10 cm, 14 cm x 14 cm, and 18 cm x 18 cm

    • For this, use the ionization chamber type 31010 included in the standard configuration of the PTW water phantom.

    • -optional field sizes:
      2 cm x 2 cm and 3 cm x 3 cm
      For this, use a dosimetry diode. PTW recommends the dosimetry diode type 60017. As an alternative, you can use the dosimetry diode T60018 or the microDiamond T60019.
  • Each depth dose curve will be saved in its own mcc file.
  • Exit the software.
