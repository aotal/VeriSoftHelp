Comparison of Dose Distribution in Volume

In case of two loaded dose volumes and the compare mode Gamma 3D, you can initiate the comparison of dose distribution in volume in the Compare pop-up window.

Both matrices must have the same unit. Otherwise, an appropriate message will be displayed and you have to calibrate the matrices first (refer to section Calibration).

  • Click the Calculate Volume Analysis button.
  • The comparison of dose distribution in volume will be calculated and the Volume Analysis dialog will be displayed:

  • With the Print button, you can print out the comparison of dose distribution in volume or you can display the preview with the Preview button. For further information, please refer to section Printing the Matrices and the Results.

After you have selected a compare class in the compare window on the Compare tab, the Export to Track-it button will be activated and you can export the comparison of the dose distribution in the volume to the Track-it database. For further information, refer to section Export to the Track-it Database.

While the Volume Analysis dialog is open, these are the options available in the other VeriSoft windows:

  • -Matrix window A or B:
    scrolling through the slices of the displayed dose volume via the Slice tab (refer to section Pop-up Windows) or by entering the slice depth in the appropriate input box
  • -Results window:
    changing the representation (refer to section Changing the Representation of the Results Window )
    showing or hiding the traffic light indicator (refer to section Pop-up Window)
  • -Compare window:
    changing the compare result representation (refer to section Changing the Visualization in the Compare Window)

It is, however, not possible to change the compare mode or the compare parameters.
