Overlaying the Patient Anatomy

Caution

Checking the dose distribution by means of the patient anatomy.

Misinterpretation!

The data processed in VeriSoft have been calculated for a homogeneous phantom and have been measured in a homogeneous phantom. These data will be overlayed over the patient anatomy without recalculation. Thus, this function only allows for an estimation of the measured data relative to the patient anatomy.

Hint

It is not possible to overlay a dose distribution over the patient anatomy in an OCTAVIUS 4D dose volume if this dose volume was calculated for one of the following cases. Appropriate error messages will be displayed.

  • -non-coplanar irradiation
  • -irradiation of a rotation unit not positioned at the isocenter
  • -merged OCTAVIUS 4D measurements
  • -composed OCTAVIUS 4D measurements

For an additional visual check of the comparison of TPS and measured data, you can load a patient anatomy in the compare window. You can overlay the patient anatomy with the dose distribution of the measurement or the failed points.

As patient data, you can use a DICOM study either loaded from an arbitrary DICOM directory or imported from the DICOM network server. The data set to load or to import should contain the modalities CT, RTDose, RTPlan, and RTStruct.
