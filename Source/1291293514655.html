Tree View of Patients in the DICOM Directory

  • These are the levels of the tree view of the patients in the DICOM Directory:
  • -Patient:
    administrative patient data
  • -DICOM Study:
    information concerning the treatment
  • -DICOM Series:
    information concerning the tests and therapy series
  • -DICOM Object:
    matrices, images, and treatment plans
    (DICOM Dir import mode only)
